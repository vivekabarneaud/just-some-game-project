# Main Story Quests — The Sundering's Shadow

These are permanent story missions that appear on the mission board, must be completed in order, and tell the overarching narrative of the game.

---

## Chapter 1: "Ashes and Dust" (Guild lvl 1)

*Theme: Survival, isolation, first hints that this land has history.*

### Mission 1: The Old Road
**Unlock:** Guild lvl 1
**Requirements:** 1 adventurer (any class)
**Difficulty:** 1 | Duration: 15 min
**Narrative:** *"Your scouts found a crumbling road that no map shows. It's ancient — the stones are carved with symbols nobody recognizes. The road leads deeper into the forest. Someone should follow it."*
**Reward:** 50 gold + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The waymarker stones bear the sigil of Lunara, goddess of knowledge. Whatever kingdom built this road, it was old when your grandparents' grandparents were young."*

### Mission 2: The Abandoned Watchtower
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 1
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 1 | Duration: 20 min
**Narrative:** *"The old road ends at a ruined watchtower. Its walls still stand, but the roof caved in long ago. From its top, your scouts can see for miles — and what they see to the south is troubling."*
**Reward:** 80 gold, 50 wood + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The watchtower logbook's last entry, centuries old: 'Day 47. The shadow spreads. The Hollow grows hungry. We cannot hold. Ferros forgive us, we are abandoning the post.' From the tower's top: dark smoke rising far to the south."*

### Mission 3: The Stranger's Cache
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 2
**Requirements:** 1 any
**Difficulty:** 1 | Duration: 10 min
**Narrative:** *"A supply cache appeared near your settlement overnight — food, medicine, tools. No footprints. No witnesses. Just a note tucked inside: 'You'll need these. Build strong. The darkness noticed you the moment you lit your first fire. — H.'"*
**Reward:** 100 food, 50 wood, 50 stone
**Lore:** *"Who is H? How do they know about your settlement? And what did they mean — the darkness noticed you?"*

## Chapter 2: "Whispers in the Dark" (Guild lvl 2)

*Theme: Growing unease, the corruption becomes real, first contact with named NPCs.*

### Mission 4: The Refugee's Warning
**Unlock:** Guild lvl 2
**Requirements:** 1 priest + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 25 min
**Narrative:** *"A half-starved family stumbles into your settlement. They fled from Millhaven, a village to the south. The mother can barely speak. The father says only: 'The dead walk. The dead walk and they don't stop.'"*
**Reward:** 100 gold, 100 food + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The refugees describe it in fragments — graves opening from below, not above. Cold that seeps into your bones. A smell like burnt metal. Their village priest tried to bless the ground. Nothing happened."*

### Mission 5: The Millhaven Expedition
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 4
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 archer
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 30 min
**Narrative:** *"You can't ignore what the refugees said. Millhaven is only two days' march south. If the dead are walking there, how long before they walk here? Your adventurers volunteer to investigate."*
**Reward:** 150 gold, 3 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Millhaven is intact but empty. Food on tables. Doors open. The cemetery is torn apart — graves opened from below. At the village center: a small shrine of black stone with fresh candles. Someone carved a single word into the altar: 'LIBERATION.'"*

### Mission 6: The Thornveil Warning
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 5
**Requirements:** 1 archer + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 20 min
**Narrative:** *"A woman emerges from the tree line at dawn. She wears green leathers and carries a bow that seems grown, not carved. She doesn't ask permission to enter. She says: 'I am Warden Kess of the Thornveil Pact. The corruption is moving north. Faster than the Elders predicted. We need to talk.'"*
**Reward:** 100 wood, 100 food + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Kess spreads a hand-drawn map on your table. The dark stain representing the Hollow Wastes has grown visibly since last season. 'Your settlement is in its path,' she says. 'You have options. But not many, and not for long.'"*

### Mission 7: The Tax Collector
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 6
**Requirements:** 1 any
**Difficulty:** 1 | Duration: 15 min
**Narrative:** *"A well-dressed bureaucrat arrives with two armed guards and a document bearing the seal of the Ashenmark Dominion. He's polite, professional, and utterly certain: your settlement falls within the 'Southern Protectorate' and is subject to taxation. He smiles a lot."*
**Reward:** 100 gold (or faction choice — TBD) + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The bureaucrat's name is Registrar Thorne. He explains that Dominion protection includes road maintenance, bandit suppression, and a garrison if needed. The price is 'a modest contribution to the collective defense.' He leaves a tax schedule. The numbers are not modest."*

## Chapter 3: "The Gathering Storm" (Guild lvl 3)

*Theme: Moral complexity, the Cult's argument, Halldora enters the story properly.*

### Mission 8: The Cult Recruiter
**Unlock:** Guild lvl 3
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 assassin or 1 archer
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 35 min
**Narrative:** *"Your adventurers are approached during a mission. A hooded figure, unarmed, hands raised. They want to talk, not fight. What they say is... unsettling. Not because it's threatening. Because it makes sense."*
**Reward:** 150 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"'The gods who blessed this land also broke it,' the figure says. 'Every shrine you build, every prayer you whisper — you're feeding the same system that created the Wastes. We offer another way. Not destruction. Liberation.' They leave a black stone pendant and vanish into the trees."*

### Mission 9: Halldora's Map
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 8
**Requirements:** 1 wizard + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 40 min
**Narrative:** *"Another raven. This time the message is longer, more urgent: 'There is a library beneath the old hills — Lunara's archive, sealed since the Sundering. The Cult found it. They're extracting texts that should never see daylight. I need those texts. You need those texts. Go now. Look for the crescent moon carved in stone. — H.'"*
**Reward:** 200 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"H is getting desperate. Whoever they are, they know about the Cult's movements before your scouts do. They know about sealed libraries that no map shows. And they know your adventurers by reputation. How?"*

### Mission 10: The Library of Lunara
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 9
**Requirements:** 1 wizard + 1 warrior + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 50 min
**Narrative:** *"The library is real. Beneath a hill marked with Lunara's crescent, your adventurers find a sealed door that opens when a wizard touches it. Inside: shelves stretching into darkness, the smell of ancient paper, and the sound of voices. Cult agents are already here."*
**Reward:** 250 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"After driving off the Cult agents, your wizard recovers a fragment of the original Court records. The text is in Old Eldoric, but some passages are clear: 'The Seventh proposes the Severance — a break between mortal and divine. The Six oppose. Netheron argues that dependence breeds weakness. He is not wrong. But his method... the cost would be beyond reckoning.' A margin note, in different handwriting: 'He was right about the problem. He was catastrophically wrong about the solution. — H.'"*

### Mission 11: The Dawn Knight
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 10
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 priest
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 25 min
**Narrative:** *"A patrol of Dawn Knights arrives at your settlement, battered and bloodied. Their captain — young, earnest, exhausted — asks for shelter. 'We've been fighting undead on the southern border for three weeks,' he says. 'There are more every day. I've lost six soldiers.' His name is Captain Aldric Bright."*
**Reward:** 200 gold, 100 food + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Over dinner, Captain Bright talks freely. He believes in the Dominion and the Radiant One, but he's troubled. 'The Archpriest says we should stop all magic use — that it feeds the Wastes. Maybe he's right. But my soldiers are dying out there, and a healing potion is the difference between a soldier who fights tomorrow and a corpse.' He pauses. 'I'd recommend building some kind of place of worship. Any worship. It keeps the Inquisitors from looking too closely at your other buildings.'"*

## Chapter 4: "The Inquisitor's Question" (Guild lvl 3-4)

*Theme: The Church's anti-magic doctrine, moral pressure, the question of whether magic helps or hurts.*

### Mission 12: The Inquisitor Arrives
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 11
**Requirements:** 1 any
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 15 min
**Narrative:** *"She arrives alone, which is somehow more intimidating than if she'd brought an army. She wears the grey cloak of the Inquisition but carries no weapon. She introduces herself as Inquisitor Maren Ashvale and asks, very politely, to see your settlement."*
**Reward:** 100 gold + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Maren doesn't threaten. She shows you maps — the Wastes' expansion over the past decade, overlaid with known magical activity. The correlation is unsettling. 'Every spell cast,' she says, 'every potion brewed, every blessing claimed — it all ripples through the Aether. And the Wastes drink those ripples.' She lets the silence do the work. 'I'm not here to punish. I'm here to ask: is your magic worth the cost?'"*

### Mission 13: Maren's Evidence
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 12
**Requirements:** 1 wizard + 1 priest
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 40 min
**Narrative:** *"Maren offers to take your adventurers to a place where the Thornveil's Primal wards meet the Wastes' edge. 'See for yourselves,' she says. 'See what "protective" magic actually does.' Your wizard and priest agree to go."*
**Reward:** 150 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"At the ward boundary, your adventurers see it clearly: the Thornveil's glowing ward-stones, pulsing with Primal energy... and the Wastes pressing against them, thicker and darker at the contact point than anywhere else. The wards are holding, but the corruption is clustering around them like moths to a flame. 'The wards work locally,' Maren says quietly. 'But globally? Every drop of magic they use to maintain them flows into the Wastes eventually. The Thornveil is winning the battle and losing the war.' Your wizard has no rebuttal."*

### Mission 14: Commander Dryven's Offer
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 13
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 assassin
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 45 min
**Narrative:** *"The Cult's military leader sends a personal invitation. Commander Dryven — the former Dawn Knight — wants to meet face to face. He promises safe passage. He also promises answers about the Library texts you recovered. 'I know what those texts say. I know what they mean. And I know why the Church is so afraid of them.'"*
**Reward:** 200 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Dryven meets you in a neutral ruin. He's older than expected, scarred, with the bearing of a soldier. He gets straight to the point: 'The Church is partially right. Magic does feed the Wastes. But stopping magic won't fix anything — the Wastes are Netheron's essence, bleeding out whether we use magic or not. The question isn't whether to use magic. It's what kind.' He shows you the tamed zone — a patch of Wastes that the Cult has stabilized using controlled Hollow magic. The corruption is still there, but contained, directed. Plants grow in the margins. 'We're not the monsters the Church says we are. We're the only ones actually trying to understand this.'"*

## Chapter 5: "Into the Hollow" (Guild lvl 4)

*Theme: Escalation, the Wastes are real and close, the dragon egg.*

### Mission 15: The Edge of the Hollow
**Unlock:** Guild lvl 4
**Requirements:** 3 adventurers (any composition), at least 1 veteran
**Difficulty:** 4 | Duration: 1 hour
**Narrative:** *"The corruption is visible from your walls now. Dead trees at the southern tree line. Animals fleeing north. Your most experienced adventurers volunteer: it's time to see how bad it really is."*
**Reward:** 300 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The Wastes are worse than anything your adventurers have seen. Trees growing downward into the earth. Water flowing uphill. Shadows that move independently. And at the boundary, a line of Thornveil ward-stones — ancient, cracking, barely holding. Beyond them: silence so deep it hurts."*

### Mission 16: Halldora's Truth
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 15
**Requirements:** 1 wizard + 1 priest + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 4 | Duration: 1 hour
**Narrative:** *"The raven comes again. But this time, the message isn't a coded note — it's a location. A cave in the eastern hills, marked with both Lunara's crescent and a personal sigil you've never seen. The note says only: 'Come. It's time we spoke face to face. Bring your smartest. — Halldora.'"*
**Reward:** 300 gold, 10 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Halldora is old. Impossibly, visibly old — her skin paper-thin, her hands trembling, her eyes milky. But when she speaks, her voice is iron. 'I was alive before the Sundering. I watched it happen. I've spent centuries trying to understand it.' She explains everything: the fragmented gods, Netheron's essence, the Wastes as a bleeding wound. Then she says something unexpected: 'The Scars are producing something. Deep in the Wastes, where the Aether bleeds thickest. Eggs. Living magic, crystallizing into... something. No one has ever retrieved one. I need you to try.'"*

### Mission 17: The Sundering Scar
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 16
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 wizard + 1 priest + 2 any (5 total)
**Difficulty:** 5 | Duration: 2 hours
**High death risk**
**Narrative:** *"Deep into the Hollow Wastes. Past the ward-stones, past the dead forest, past the silent zone where even the wind stops. Your adventurers find the Scar — a fissure in the earth that glows with raw Aether. The air hums. The ground pulses like a heartbeat. And there, nestled in crystallized magic: an egg. Warm. Alive. Waiting."*
**Reward:** DRAGON EGG + 500 gold + 15 astral shards
**Lore:** *"As your adventurers lift the egg, the Wastes seem to... notice. The ground trembles. The silence breaks. A sound like a breath, drawn after centuries. Something has changed. Something fundamental. Your adventurers run. The egg pulses against their hands, warm as a living thing. When they cross back past the ward-stones, the egg flares once — bright enough to see for miles. Everyone will know what you found. Everyone."*

## Chapter 6: "The Dragon's Dawn" (Guild lvl 4-5)

*Theme: The egg hatches, every faction reacts, the world changes.*

### Mission 18: The Dominion's Demand
**Unlock:** Dragon egg hatched
**Requirements:** 1 warrior + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 30 min
**Narrative:** *"Captain Bright returns — but this time he's not asking for shelter. He carries a sealed letter from High King Aldren himself. The Dominion knows about the dragon. They want it. The letter is polite. The implication is not."*
**Reward:** 200 gold + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"The letter: 'The creature you harbor is a concentration of the very energy that poisons our land. The Church has determined it poses an existential threat. We request — firmly — its transfer to Dominion custody for study and containment. Failure to comply will be noted.' Captain Bright looks uncomfortable. 'I'm sorry,' he says. 'I told them you could be trusted. They didn't listen.'"*

### Mission 19: The Thornveil's Plea
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 18
**Requirements:** 1 priest + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 30 min
**Narrative:** *"Elder Rowan himself travels to your settlement. The first time he's left the forest in decades. He asks only one thing: to see the dragon."*
**Reward:** 200 gold, 200 food + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"Rowan kneels before the hatchling. His eyes fill with tears. 'This creature,' he whispers. 'It's made of the same energy as the Wastes. But it's alive. It chose life.' He looks up at you. 'I don't know what this means. None of my knowledge prepared me for this. But I know it matters. Raise it well. The world is watching.'"*

### Mission 20: The Hierophant's Message
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 19
**Requirements:** 1 assassin + 1 any
**Difficulty:** 3 | Duration: 35 min
**Narrative:** *"A message arrives by means nobody can explain — it simply appears on your table, written on black paper in silver ink. It is from the Hierophant."*
**Reward:** 200 gold, 5 astral shards + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"'Your dragon was born from destruction. Like all of us. Like freedom itself. The Church will tell you it's dangerous. The druids will tell you it's sacred. The Dominion will tell you it's theirs. They are all wrong. It is simply new. And new things terrify those who profit from the old. Raise it without chains. — The Hierophant.' Postscript: 'We are not your enemy. But we will be, if you let them put a collar on it.'"*

### Mission 21: Maren's Warning
**Unlock:** Complete Mission 20
**Requirements:** 1 any
**Difficulty:** 2 | Duration: 15 min
**Narrative:** *"Inquisitor Ashvale returns. She is not polite this time. She is afraid."*
**Reward:** 100 gold + Lore Fragment
**Lore:** *"'Do you understand what you've done?' Maren's composure cracks for the first time. 'That creature is made of concentrated Aether. Every breath it takes sends ripples through the magical fabric. It's a living Sundering Scar.' She steadies herself. 'I'm not going to threaten you. But the Archpriest will. And he has the Order of the Dawn behind him. You need to decide what this dragon is worth to you. Because the price is going up.'"*
